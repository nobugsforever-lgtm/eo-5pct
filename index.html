5% 准备小提示 · Prep Tips
  • 感受 · Feelings：使用真实而丰富的词汇 — 不是"压力大"，而是"被压垮"、"焦虑"、"无能为力"。Use precise, rich vocabulary — not "stressed," but "overwhelmed," "anxious," "helpless."
  • 标题 · Headline：只用一句话。不超过约 15 个字。One sentence only. Around 15 characters max.
  • 意义 · Significance：这才是 80% 的重点。深入挖掘 — "为什么对我重要？" "我从中认识到什么？"This is the 80%. Dig deep — "Why does this matter to me?" "What do I realize about myself?"
  • 切勿沉浸在叙述故事中。专注于 5%。Don't get lost in story. Focus on the 5%.
